About Me

I'm an avid reader who is constantly pushing myself to learn new things. I switched to reading exclusively on my iPad due to running out of space for all of my books. Picture in your head the opening scenes of Belle from Disney's Beauty and the Beast and your would have a good idea of how I am most of the time. When I'm not reading on my iPad, I'm either playing on it, listening to music on it, or working.


My husband and I have two fur-babies, Lilac and Giselle. They are 17 year old cats that allow us to reside with them. They are quick to let us know when our work takes too much of their time as they require a certain amount of quality lap time each day.
